Article Title

In Preston, the average percentage of the sky covered by clouds experiences significant seasonal variation over the course of the year. The clearer part of the year in Preston begins around June 5 and lasts for 4.4 months, ending around October 19.The clearest month of the year in Preston is July, during which on average the sky is clear, mostly clear, or partly cloudy 79% of the time. The cloudier part of the year begins around October 19 and lasts for 7.6 months, ending around June 5.The cloudiest month of the year in Preston is January, during which on average the sky is overcast or mostly cloudy 55% of the time.
